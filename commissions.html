commission status: open

Commission requests for individual clients are open.
Incoming inquiries will be responded to as soon as possible.

For large-scale inquiries, such as soundtrack work, please contact me through email with an overview of your project and we can discuss independently.

guidelines

  • Be clear with what you want; provide context, a vision, and musical references. I want to help provide you with the music that you want, so be detailed!
  • For specific media, such as with videos and animation, storyboards, animatics or even just a short verbal run-down would be very helpful!
  • Additional fees will be added for commercial usage and rush work (1-2 week turnaround), please include the purpose of your inquiry and your final deadline.
  • Additionally, for any arrangement/remix work, please ensure you are able to secure the necessary rights or licenses if you plan on using the work commercially. Inquire at your own discretion.

soundcirclet@gmail.com

commission services

original BGM (for vtubers, streams, videos, etc.)

  • 1 piece of custom looping background music
  • Starting from 1-2 minutes in length, with an additional rate per minute.

vocal song production

  • 1 fully composed & produced song between 3-4 minutes in length
  • Full mix and master, including vocal timing and tuning
  • Melody + harmony guides for the main melody (MIDI and audio)
  • Lyrics are NOT included, but lyric-writing services can be added for an additional cost.

instrumental remake/arrangement

  • 1 remake or rearrangement of an existing song's instrumental for use in vocal covers
  • Starting from 1-2 minutes in length, with an additional rate per minute.
